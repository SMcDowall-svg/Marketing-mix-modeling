# Marketing-mix-modeling\

This repository is a simulated case study demonstrating marketing mixed modeling (MMM). 

**Subject** 

Media Budget Allocation for Beauty Brands

**Context**

A portfolio of 5 beauty brands with data for each brand over a 3-year period (2020-2022):
*	Monthly media budget
*	Monthly revenue
*	Monthly market share
*	Information on the global market and competition

The company wants to optimize the allocation of its total media budget for the year 2023 across these 5 brands.

**Data**

"Raw Data" tab: Monthly information per brand (2020-2022)
*	Media budget
*	Revenue
*	Market share
"Market" tab: Monthly information on the global market and competition
"2023 Budget" tab: 
*	Total media budget to be allocated for 2023

**Goal**

Determine the best allocation of the total media budget among the 5 brands to maximize overall return on investment, based on mathematical criteria.
